BITSMUN

BITSMUN Goa is one of the fastest growing Model United Nation conferences in the country.

Held in one of the most scenic destinations in India, we have continued to see increased participation over the past few years. We maintain a blog as a means to keep the participants interested and engage their grey matter throughout the year.

BITSMUN Goa is a completely student organised affair, jointly and ably led by our Director General and Secretary General. They are in charge of a small team that works all year round, with dedication and passion to make this 3 day gala of politics, fantasy and everything in between a roaring success.
